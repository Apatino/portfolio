it's you, guaranteed.

Identity assurance through trusted documents and biometrics.

your.id works by fusing together six different types of identity data into one identity quotient (idQ).
idQ is the certainty score that an individual is the stated natural person.

Linking physical documentation to web identity

idQis a fusion of six types of identity data: biographic, biometric, technical/environmental, behavioral, social, and documents.
Individuals own their idQ and can adjust or delete it at any time.

Features and Benefits

  • Tamper checks ensure document integrity.

  • Fewer passwords to remember and credentials to manage.

  • Easy integration with custom apps.

  • Live biometrics agains physical ID.

Once an idQ is created, an individual can share their idQ with an enterprise, a government, or even another individual (i.e., a relying party) instead of disclosing their underlying identity data.

Consumers use your.id to easily and reliably prove their identity, auto-fill website forms, and conduct transactions. All with the confidence that their identity is secure.

Consumers
