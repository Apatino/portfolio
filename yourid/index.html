It works by fusing together six different types of identity data into an identity quotient (idQ),
which is the certainty score that an individual is the stated natural person.

idQ - Fusing Identity Data

idQ is a fusion of six types of identity data: biometric, biographic, trusted documents, social, behavioural and technical/environmental.
An individual owns their idQ and can delete it at any time: your.id is its secure custodian.

It Works at Every Level

Consumers use your.id to easily and reliably prove their identity, auto-fill website forms, and conduct transactions. All with the confidence that their identity is secure.

Consumers

Retailers can implement your.id in their brick and mortar establishments as well as their online presence to protect themselves and their customers from fraudulent purchases. Associates no longer need to examine and validate consumer identification documentation.

Retailers

Clients in the financial sector can use your.id to verify the identity of account owners and beneficiaries. By ensuring the identity of customers, financial transactions are more secure, and fraud is reduced.

Financial Institutions
